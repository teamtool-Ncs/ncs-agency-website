About NCS Bharat

We are Namdhari Consultancy Service — a growth-focused digital marketing agency helping brands scale with AI-driven strategies and creative execution.

Our Story

Born to Solve the Real Marketing Problem

NCS Bharat was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between businesses and genuine digital growth. We saw too many brands investing heavily in marketing yet getting little to no return — not because marketing doesn't work, but because the strategies were misaligned.

We built Namdhari Consultancy Service to change that. By combining human creativity with the power of Artificial Intelligence and data analytics, we create marketing strategies that don't just look good — they deliver measurable, real-world results.

Today, NCS Bharat is trusted by 100+ brands globally, from startups to established enterprises, across industries like education, eCommerce, healthcare, and more.
